Crystals Collector

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score with the random number. You lose the game by going over the random number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time the game starts it will change the values of each crystal.
